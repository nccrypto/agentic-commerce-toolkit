# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, technically honest, and constructive. Separate observed facts from interpretation; disclose conflicts where relevant; do not pressure contributors to reveal private wallet, identity, or commercial information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, doxxing, credential solicitation, deceptive promotion, fabricated results, undisclosed paid advocacy, or attempts to move unsafe financial actions into examples are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate these expectations. A dedicated private conduct contact will be added before accepting broad external contributions.
